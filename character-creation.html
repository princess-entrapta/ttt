Character Creation

Combat characteristics determination mechanics

Unlike other games who proposes a class system or building with stats, the way TTT Rules proposes to build character is to use a card draft.

The proposed way to build characters is the following:

  • The Game Master prepares sets of cards of every Abilities. There must be 1 set for every 2 players.
  • The Game Master shuffles each set, then deals, to every player cards, face down, from the first set until there is none, then card from the next sets, and continue dealing until every player has 6 cards.
  • Every player pick any card, and freely exchange any previously picked cards with their current hand. They can never have more than 4 cards. Their hand and cards must remain hidden.
  • They give their hand to the player on their left. The process is repeated until two full table turns are done. So, for 5 players, they repeat the process 10 times total.

Players are then invited to pick any set of base Weapons and Armors (no bonus). This constitutes their base character.

Non Combat Characteristics

Skills

Characters must chose 1 area of mastery and 1 area of proficiency, among the following Skills that will give them bonus to doing certain Actions And Reactions:

  • Speech. This is used to deceive / persuade / distract NPCs.
  • Resource. This is used to scavenge / bargain.
  • Perception. This is used for acuity / guessing intentions / search.
  • Athletics. This is used for running / climbing / pushing.
  • Stealth. This is used for hiding / general stealing.
  • Science. This is used for first aid / general knowledge.

Stress Characteristic

Finally, they must chose a a line to describe a stress characteristic of their choice. This trait can be activated by the Game Master when a character is [Under Stress](Stress#Under Stress).

  • Irritable. I am prone to getting angry when frustrated by a social setting
  • Pessimistic. I give up very easily.
  • Distracted. I am prone to be thinking something else than what I'm currently doing
  • Spender. I relieve by giving myself the shiniest stuff.
  • Low esteem. I belittle myself unders stress.

The Game Master can force you to trigger them whenever your character is under Stress, if they find fitting their setting. Check the Stress page for their effect.
